Don't have a wasteof account?

You need to use the wasteof website to create a wasteof account, then you can log in here.

Want to sign in with Google or GitHub?

You can't sign in using Google or GitHub on third party wasteof clients such as Tweetof. Log into wasteof.money, add a password to your account, and then you can log in here.

What is Tweetof?

Tweetof (or tweetof.money) is an unofficial wasteof.money client designed to resemble Twitter circa 2015.
